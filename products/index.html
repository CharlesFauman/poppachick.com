Our Exciting Range of Products

At Poppachick, we pride ourselves in combining two timeless snacks – popcorn and chicken bites – into a unique, mouth-watering experience. Each of our distinct flavor profiles offers a treat for every palate.

Original Poppachick

Experience the classic that started the Poppachick revolution. Delight in the signature blend of popcorn and air-fried chicken bites, carefully seasoned with our secret array of spices.

Poppachick Fire

Embark on a fiery journey with Poppachick Fire. This variant takes our Original Poppachick and infuses it with an exhilarating heat. Not for the faint-hearted, but a thrill for the spice enthusiast!

Poppachick Sweet n’ Spicy

Indulge in a whirlwind of flavors with Poppachick Sweet n’ Spicy. This version offers a harmonious blend of sweet and spicy that dances on your taste buds, ensuring an unforgettable snacking session.

We are always in pursuit of the next tantalizing combination, experimenting with flavors to quench your snack cravings. Stay tuned for more exciting, delectable products!
