About Us

Poppachick was founded on the principle of creating unique and delicious snacks. We saw the joy popcorn brought to moviegoers and the delight spicy air-fried chicken brought to food enthusiasts, and thought, “why not combine them?” That’s how Poppachick was born. We’re committed to producing high-quality, flavorful snacks that surprise and satisfy with every bite.
